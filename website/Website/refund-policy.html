Refund Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Overview

At PBooksPro, we stand behind the quality of our software and want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This Refund Policy outlines our refund process for PBooksPro software licenses.

2. Free Trial Period

PBooksPro offers a 30-day free trial period for all license tiers. During this trial period, you can fully evaluate the software without any financial commitment. We encourage you to thoroughly test the software during this period to ensure it meets your needs before making a purchase.

3. 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you may request a full refund within 14 days of your purchase date. You do not need to provide a reason for your refund request.

To be eligible for a refund, your refund request must be submitted within 14 days of the original purchase date.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us using one of the following methods:

  • Email: support@pbookspro.com
  • Website: Contact Page

Please include your purchase receipt or order number to help us process your refund request quickly.

5. Refund Processing

Refunds will be processed without undue delay and will be completed within 14 days of receiving your refund request.

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. The refund amount will be the full purchase price.

Since we use Paddle as our payment processor, refunds are subject to Paddle's refund processing times and policies. The refund will appear in your account according to your payment method provider's processing times (typically 5-10 business days for credit cards, may vary for other payment methods).

You will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been processed.

6. License Revocation

Upon processing of a refund, your license to use PBooksPro will be revoked. You must stop using the software and uninstall it from all devices.

7. Contact Information

For refund requests or questions about this policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@pbookspro.com
  • Website: Contact Page

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Governing Law

This Refund Policy is governed by the laws of Pakistan and is subject to our Terms of Service. Any disputes regarding refunds will be resolved in accordance with these terms and applicable law.
