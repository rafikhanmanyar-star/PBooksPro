Refund Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Overview

At PBooksPro, we stand behind the quality of our software and want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which refunds may be granted for PBooksPro software licenses.

2. Free Trial Period

PBooksPro offers a 30-day free trial period for all license tiers. During this trial period, you can fully evaluate the software without any financial commitment. We encourage you to thoroughly test the software during this period to ensure it meets your needs before making a purchase.

3. Refund Eligibility

Refund requests may be considered under the following circumstances:

3.1 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you may request a full refund within 30 days of your purchase date, provided that:

  • The refund request is submitted within 30 days of the original purchase date
  • You have not violated our Terms of Service
  • The software has not been used for commercial purposes beyond reasonable evaluation
  • You provide a valid reason for the refund request

3.2 Technical Issues

If you experience technical issues that prevent you from using the software and our support team is unable to resolve them within a reasonable timeframe, you may be eligible for a refund. We will make every effort to resolve technical issues before processing a refund.

3.3 Duplicate Purchases

If you accidentally purchase multiple licenses or make a duplicate purchase, we will refund the duplicate purchase(s) upon verification.

4. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are generally not eligible for refunds:

  • Purchases made more than 30 days ago
  • Licenses that have been transferred or resold
  • Purchases where the Terms of Service have been violated
  • Refund requests made after the software has been used extensively for commercial purposes
  • Maintenance or support subscriptions (if purchased separately)

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our support team at support@pbookspro.com or through our Contact Page
  2. Include your purchase receipt or order number
  3. Provide a brief explanation of why you are requesting a refund
  4. Allow 3-5 business days for our team to review your request

6. Refund Processing

6.1 Review Process

All refund requests will be reviewed by our support team. We may contact you for additional information to better understand your situation and to ensure we can improve our product based on your feedback.

6.2 Approval and Processing Time

If your refund request is approved:

  • Refunds will be processed within 5-10 business days of approval
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase
  • The refund amount will be the full purchase price (excluding any transaction fees that may be charged by payment processors)
  • You will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been processed

6.3 Payment Processor

Since we use Paddle as our payment processor, refunds are subject to Paddle's refund processing times and policies. The refund will appear in your account according to your payment method provider's processing times (typically 5-10 business days for credit cards, may vary for other payment methods).

7. License Revocation

Upon approval of a refund, your license to use PBooksPro will be revoked. You must:

  • Stop using the software immediately
  • Uninstall the software from all devices
  • Delete any copies of the software in your possession

Continued use of the software after a refund has been processed may result in legal action.

8. Partial Refunds

In certain circumstances, we may offer partial refunds instead of full refunds. This may occur if:

  • The software has been used for a significant period before the refund request
  • There are extenuating circumstances that warrant a partial refund
  • You have received value from support services or training

Any partial refund offer will be clearly communicated to you before processing.

9. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback through your bank or credit card company instead of contacting us directly, we reserve the right to dispute the chargeback and provide evidence of the purchase and our refund policy. We encourage you to contact us first to resolve any issues, as we are committed to finding a fair solution.

10. Special Circumstances

We understand that special circumstances may arise. If you believe your situation warrants special consideration, please contact us with details. We will review each case individually and may make exceptions to our standard refund policy at our discretion.

11. Currency and Exchange Rates

Refunds will be processed in the same currency as the original purchase. If currency exchange rates have changed between the purchase date and refund date, the refund amount may differ slightly due to exchange rate fluctuations. We are not responsible for any differences caused by exchange rate changes.

12. Contact Information

For refund requests or questions about this policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@pbookspro.com
  • Subject Line: "Refund Request - [Your Order Number]"
  • Website: Contact Page

Please include your order number, purchase date, and reason for the refund request to expedite the process.

13. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of PBooksPro after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Governing Law

This Refund Policy is governed by the laws of Pakistan and is subject to our Terms of Service. Any disputes regarding refunds will be resolved in accordance with these terms and applicable law.
